About Me

I had finished an eight months coding Bootcamp by the organization Thinkful. It was a fast-paced program focused on full-stack web development. While I learned a tremendous amount of knowledge, I wanted to solidify my foundations before I started any job. This led me to Year Up, a skill development program that aims to train young individuals to be professionals in the tech world.

I hope to work as a software developer in a company that aims to make a positive change. Once I am financially stable, I would love to teach the valuable lesson I've learned professionally and personally to the up-and-coming youths of the world.

Something to know about me is that when I get passionate about something, I dive deep into that passion. Whether it be photography, music, movies, or programming, I take the time and effort to appreciate my endeavors in-depth. I hope to continue my ever-expanding array of hobbies and interests.
